About Us

Our mission is to revolutionize parking management for modern cities and facilities using cutting-edge AI technology. The Smart Parking Management System leverages YOLO-based object detection to provide real-time, accurate information about parking availability, making it easier for drivers to find spots and for facilities to optimize their resources. We serve educational institutions, hospitals, shopping centers, and urban parking providers, offering a scalable, cost-effective, and eco-friendly solution that reduces congestion, saves time, and supports sustainable urban mobility.

Privacy Policy

We value your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information. Our system processes images from parking facilities to detect available spaces, but we implement strict controls to safeguard all data. Images and user information are encrypted during transmission and storage. We do not use personal data to train our AI models without explicit consent, and access to sensitive data is restricted by role-based permissions. Users have the right to request access, correction, or deletion of their data. For more details or to exercise your rights, please contact our support team.
